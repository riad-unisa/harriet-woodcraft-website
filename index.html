Welcome

Welcome to Harriet Boone Woodcraft. Harriet creates handmade wooden jewellery including earrings, brooches, bracelets, and hairpins. Each piece is carefully designed and crafted using natural materials and traditional woodworking techniques.

About the Craft

Harriet combines creativity with craftsmanship to produce unique wearable art. Her work focuses on natural wood textures, simple elegant shapes, and sustainable handmade production.
