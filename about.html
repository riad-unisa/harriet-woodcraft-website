About Harriet

Harriet Boone is an artisan woodworker who specialises in creating handmade wooden jewellery. Her work includes earrings, brooches, bracelets, and hairpins crafted from natural wood materials.

After moving to Adelaide, Harriet decided to transform her creative hobby into a small business. Her goal is to share her creative process through tutorials and progress videos while selling her handmade items both locally and online.

Each piece is carefully designed and produced in her small home workshop using woodworking tools and sustainable materials.
